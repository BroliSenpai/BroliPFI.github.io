What are the benefits of watching "Anime"?

You learn about Japanese culture.

The Japanese culture is beautiful.

You learn about Japanese food.

One of their iconic food is Ramen.

You learn life lessons.

Too much arrogance leads to losses eventually.

You learn about relationships.

Naruto and Sasuke's relationship in the anime Naruto.

You learn about hard work, patience, and team work.

Megumi and Maki's teamwork in Jujutsu Kaisen.

You learn about the ugly side of human nature.

A noble household's doing in Akame Ga Kill.

You learn how to have fun.

Fairy Tail teaches you how to have fun and relax.

You learn unexpected things.

If you watch School Live, you learn about mental health issues like PTSD.
